FAQ

Common questions, honest answers.

How long does a typical project take?

It depends on the scope. A business website typically takes 5–10 days. A web app or e-commerce project usually takes 2–4 weeks. I provide a clear timeline estimate before work begins so you always know what to expect.

Do you work with clients outside Nepal?

Yes — I work with international clients regularly. Communication is via WhatsApp, email, or video call. Payments can be arranged in NPR or USD depending on your location.

What do I need to provide to get started?

Basic requirements: your business name, goals, preferred style (you can share reference sites), and any text/images you want to include. I guide you through the rest — you do not need to be technical.

Will I be able to update the site myself?

Yes. If you opt for a CMS build (WordPress or similar), you can update text, images, and pages without touching code. I also provide a short handover session so you feel confident managing your site.

What is your payment structure?

Typically 50% upfront to confirm the project and begin work, and 50% upon delivery before the site goes live. For larger projects, milestone-based payments can be arranged.

Do you provide hosting and domain services?

I can recommend and set up hosting and domain registration for you, or deploy to your existing hosting. My Cloud Deployment service covers the full setup — server, SSL, domain, and going live.
